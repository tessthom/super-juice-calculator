Supplies

To make a batch of Super Juice, you'll need the following on hand:

  • Citric acid (Can be found at specialty grocers or online)
  • Malic acid (It's possible to make super juice with just citric acid. Some prefer a lemon super juice to only have citric acid, so it's not the end of the world if you proceed without. Just adjust your citric acid to make up the full total acid percentage for the citrus.)
  • A non-reactive container that can be sealed
  • Cheesecloth or a nut milk bag
  • An immersion/stick blender or standard blender
  • A peeler
  • A juicing implement of some kind
